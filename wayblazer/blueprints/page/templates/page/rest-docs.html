{# EMPLOYEES#}

Employees

localhost:8000/api/employee

Parameters:

  • company

    Filters by company employee is associated with

  • exclude_state

    Excludes employees from a certain state (i.e. TX, LA, CA)

  • email_provider

    Filters employees by their email provider (i.e. gmail, yahoo)

  • limit

    Selects the amount of employees you wish to list in the JSON response (default: 10)

  • offset

    Selects the amount of employees you wish to offset in the response (default: 0)

Examples:

Searching by company

This endpoint searches for employees in the "Rapid Trading Intl" company with a result limit of 10 employees per request and starting on the 21st employee. Offset and Limit are explicitly set.
